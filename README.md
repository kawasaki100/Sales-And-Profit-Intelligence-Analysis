### Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing business performance using sales data across multiple countries, products, and customer segments. The goal was to transform raw transactional data into a decision-making dashboard that highlights revenue trends, profitability, and growth patterns.

### Data Sources

**Sales Data**: The primary dataset used for this analysis is the sales and profit intelligence data.xlsx, containing detailed information about each sale made by the company.

### Business Problem
Organizations often struggle to answer key questions like:
Which products are truly profitable?
Are discounts improving or hurting performance?
Where is revenue growing or declining?

This project addresses these challenges by providing a centralized analytics dashboard for performance tracking

### Tools & Skills Used

- **Microsoft Excel** - Data Cleaning
- **SQL Server** - Data Analysis
- **Microsoft Power BI** - Data Modeling and Visualization
- DAX (for calculated Measures)
  
### Dataset Description
- The dataset includes:
- Sales revenue
- Profit and cost (COGS)
- Units sold
- Discounts applied
- Product categories
- Customer segments (e.g., Government)
- Geographic data (countries)
- Time data (month, year)

### Data Cleaning/Preparation

In the initial data preparation phase, we performed the following tasks:
1. Data loading and inspection
2. Removing duplicates
3. Standardization
4. Handling missing values

### Exploratory Data Analysis
EDA involved exploring the sales data to answer key questions, such as:

- Where is money coming from?
- What should we sell more of?
- Who are our customers?
- When do we get the most sales?
- Are discounts hurting us?

### Key Metrics (DAX Measures)
Some of the core metrics developed include:
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin (%)
- Discount %
- Year-over-Year (YoY) Growth

### Dashboard Features
The dashboard includes:
#### KPI Summary
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin
- Units Sold
#### Visual Analysis
- Sales by Country
- Profit by Product
- Sales Trend Over Time
- Segment Distribution
- Discount vs Profit Relationship
#### Interactivity
- Filters for Country, Product, Segment, and Year

### Key Insights
From the analysis:
- Discounts drives sales
- Sales show clear seasonal trends across months
- Some regions outperform others in both revenue and profitability
- The Government is our biggest customer
- The business grew by over 300% in one year

### Business Recommendations
Based on insights:
- Optimize discount strategy to protect profit margins
- Focus on high-margin products for growth
- Target high-performing regions for expansion
- Invest in marketing and promotions during peak sales seasons to maximize revenue.
- Reduce reliance on low-profit segments
